Mission Statement

The purpose of Eternal Kings Motorcycle Club is to build a men's MC and a brotherhood of men that share a love for riding motorcycles, having barbecues and enjoying life.

We are 100% diverse and welcome members of every ethnicity. We respect and value the MC protocol. Our motto is "Leave Your Mark" as we believe we as a brotherhood of Kings should strive to be remembered by our actions. We look forward to connecting with you.
